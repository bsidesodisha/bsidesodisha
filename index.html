About The Event

Welcome to BSides Odisha, a premier cybersecurity event held annually in the beautiful state of Odisha, India. We bring together cybersecurity professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts from around the world to foster knowledge sharing, collaboration, and community engagement.


At BSides Odisha, we believe in the power of community-driven learning and networking. Our conference provides a unique platform for attendees to explore the latest trends, innovations, and best practices in cybersecurity. Through insightful talks, hands-on workshops, interactive discussions, and networking opportunities, participants gain valuable insights and forge meaningful connections with like-minded individuals.


Our event showcases cutting-edge research, emerging technologies, and practical solutions to address the ever-evolving challenges in cybersecurity. We encourage active participation and engagement, allowing attendees to share their expertise, experiences, and perspectives.

Explore Odisha

"If you are traveling to Odisha for the first time, you're in for a treat! Odisha, located on the eastern coast of India, is a land of diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and rich heritage. Here are some amazing tourist destinations that you can visit:"

- Visit the Lingaraj Temple, one of the most important Hindu temples in India.

- Explore the Dhauli Hills, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that is home to several Buddhist monuments.

- Take a walk through the Ekamra Kanan Botanical Gardens, one of the largest botanical gardens in India.

-Visit the Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves, a group of 18 caves that contain Hindu and Jain sculptures.

- Visit the Orissa State Museum, which houses a collection of artifacts from the history of Odisha.

- Take a boat ride on the Bindu Sarovara, a sacred lake in the heart of Bhubaneswar

- Visit the Jagannath Temple, one of the most important Hindu pilgrimage sites in India
