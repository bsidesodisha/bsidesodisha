About CTF At BSides Odisha

As part of the BSides Odisha 2023 event, we are excited to host an engaging and challenging Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. The CTF is designed to provide participants with an opportunity to test their cybersecurity skills, solve real-world challenges, and compete in a fun and educational environment.

CTF Categories

The CTF challenges will cover a wide range of categories, including but not limited to:

- Web exploitation

- Reverse engineering

- Cryptography

- Forensics

- Binary exploitation

- Steganography

- Network analysis

- Exploit development

December 2, 2023


Who Can Participate

" The CTF is open to cybersecurity enthusiasts, students, professionals, and anyone interested in testing their skills. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a beginner, there will be challenges suitable for different skill levels. "

December 2, 2023


Format

The CTF will feature a Jeopardy-style format, where participants will need to solve challenges to earn points. Each challenge will have a specific point value based on its difficulty level. The participant or team with the highest score at the end of the competition will be declared the winner.

December 2, 2023


Prizes

We're offering exciting prizes for the top participants or teams, including cash prizes, exclusive cybersecurity tools, and recognition in the cybersecurity community.

December 2, 2023


  • Participation and Registration

    - Participation is free and open to all registered attendees of BSides Odisha 2023.

    - Participants can register for the CTF on-site during the event or through the event website in advance.

  • How to Submit

    Learning and Collaboration

    The CTF not only offers a competitive environment but also serves as a learning opportunity. Participants can collaborate, share knowledge, and learn from others while solving challenges.
