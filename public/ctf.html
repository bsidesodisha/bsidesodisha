About BSides Odisha Web3CTF

Dive into the cutting-edge world of Web3 security with the BSides Odisha Web3CTF, a dynamic and educational competition designed to challenge and enhance your cybersecurity skills. Whether you're a seasoned professional or a curious enthusiast, this CTF is your gateway to exploring the intricate landscape of decentralized technologies.

CTF Categories

The CTF challenges will cover a wide range of categories, including but not limited to:

- Web exploitation

- Reverse engineering

- Cryptography

- Forensics

- Binary exploitation

- Steganography

- Network analysis

- Exploit development

November 30th at 8:00 PM IST - December 2, 2023 at 2:00 PM IST


Who Can Participate

" Whether you're a cybersecurity professional, student, or an enthusiast eager to explore the world of Web3 security, this CTF is for you! All are welcome to participate and expand their skills. "

November 30th at 8:00 PM IST - December 2, 2023 at 2:00 PM IST


Why Participate

- Cutting-Edge Challenges:

Engage in hands-on challenges that encompass smart contract vulnerabilities, blockchain security, and decentralized application (DApp) security.

- Networking Opportunities:

Connect with industry experts, fellow cybersecurity enthusiasts, and like-minded individuals, fostering collaboration and knowledge exchange.

- Educational Insights:

Stay ahead of the curve by gaining valuable insights into the latest trends, vulnerabilities, and defensive strategies in the evolving realm of Web3 security.
November 30th at 8:00 PM IST - December 2, 2023 at 2:00 PM IST


Format

The CTF will feature a Jeopardy-style format, where participants will need to solve challenges to earn points. Each challenge will have a specific point value based on its difficulty level. The participant or team with the highest score at the end of the competition will be declared the winner.

November 30th at 8:00 PM IST - December 2, 2023 at 2:00 PM IST


Prizes

We're offering exciting prizes for the top participants or teams, including cash prizes, exclusive cybersecurity tools, and recognition in the cybersecurity community.

- EJPT V2 Exam Voucher, CTF Winner Trophy, and two 6-month Pentester Lab subscriptions.

- CTF Winner Trophy and two 3-month Pentester Lab subscriptions.

- CTF Winner Medal and two 1-month Pentester Lab subscriptions.

November 30th at 8:00 PM IST - December 2, 2023 at 2:00 PM IST
