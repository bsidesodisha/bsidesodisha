Call For Bounty Track

Welcome to the Call for Bounty Track (CFB) for BSides Odisha 2023! Our event is all about celebrating the ever-evolving world of cybersecurity, and the CFB is your opportunity to shine. We invite ethical hackers, security researchers, and bug bounty enthusiasts to be part of our mission to secure the digital landscape.

    What is the Bounty Track?

    The CFB is a dedicated platform for bug bounty hunters and security experts. It's your stage to demonstrate your skills, share your discoveries, contribute to the security community, and gain well-deserved recognition.

How to Participate

- Submit Your Bug Bounty Reports: Share your findings and reports for review.

- Share Your Knowledge: Present your insights and expertise through presentations and workshops.

- Network: Connect with fellow bug bounty hunters and cybersecurity professionals.



Why Participate

- Showcase Your Skills: Put your expertise on display.

- Collaborate: Join forces with like-minded individuals.

- Gain Recognition: Receive acknowledgment for your contributions.

- Secure the Digital Landscape: Play your part in making the digital world safer.

- Global Exposure: Have the chance to present your findings to a global audience.

December 2, 2023


Submission Deadline

The deadline for CFB submissions is 10 November 2023. All submissions will be reviewed by a panel of experts in the cybersecurity field. Selected speakers will be notified by 14 November to 20 November 2023.

    How to Submit

    Submit your proposal through our online submission form: Online Submission Form

    We look forward to receiving your proposals and creating an exceptional educational and networking experience at BSides Odisha 2023. Join us in shaping the future of cybersecurity!

    For inquiries or more information, please contact info@bsidesodisha.in.
